What to bring

Please bring yourself, and some snacks and/or drinks to share. (Maybe a cooler and a blanket too? Definitely sunscreen.)

The Parks Department says we're not allowed to bring alcoholic beverages. (There are also more rules.)

our registry

Getting there

Public transit

The park is closest to the 145th Street stop on the 1, but the A,C,D, and B are only one block further (also 145th). The BX19 and M11 also stop directly in the park.

Handy References

  • MTA website (has a trip planner!)

Parking

Parking is available, but it's underneath the park. You may be able to find on-street parking in the area, but if you can't or want to park under the park, you must get a parking permit from the attendant at the 145th street entrance booth, and theres a shuttle from the parking lot back to the park (both are free).

Google map directions to get from the entrance booth to the parking lot.

Where to pee

There's a rec facility in the center of the park that has bathrooms near the bus drop off.
